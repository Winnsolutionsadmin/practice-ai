## Qualification Criteria (Score 0–10)

### Positive Signals (add points)
| Signal | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Independent practice (not hospital-owned) | +3 |
| 1–8 providers | +2 |
| Google rating 3.8–4.6 (not perfect = has real issues) | +1 |
| Reviews mention wait times or scheduling problems | +2 |
| Reviews mention "hard to reach" or "office doesn't call back" | +2 |
| Active Google Business profile (posts, photos) | +1 |
| 50–500 Google reviews (established but not enterprise) | +1 |
| Practice has been open 3+ years | +1 |
| Website looks outdated or has no online booking | +2 |

### Disqualifying Signals (skip)
- Part of hospital system or large group (10+ providers)
- Concierge/direct-pay only (different economics)
- Reviews mention "great scheduling" or "always on time" (already solved)
- Less than 1 year in business (too early)
- Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) — different funding model

### Minimum Threshold
Score 6+/10 before including in outreach list.

## Priority Segments

### Tier 1 (Highest Priority)
- Dental practices 2–4 dentists, 3–4.5 stars, 100–400 reviews
- Family medicine 2–5 providers, independent, established 5+ years
- Chiropractic practices with multiple locations
- Dermatology — high appointment volume, strong recall economics

### Tier 2
- Mental health group practices (2+ therapists)
- Pediatrics — high volume, parent communication complexity
- OB/GYN private practices
- Physical therapy — high no-show rate, insurance-heavy

### Tier 3 (Lower urgency, approach in Month 2+)
- Single-provider practices (smaller deal, harder ROI case)
- Practices with <50 Google reviews (may not have the volume)
- Practices using paper-based systems only (longer sales cycle)

## Data Sources for Contact Info

1. **Google Business Profile** — phone number, website
2. **Practice website** → Contact/About page for owner/manager name
3. **LinkedIn** — direct decision-maker contact
4. **California Medical Board** (license lookup) → confirms provider and practice address
5. **California Dental Board** (license lookup) → same
6. **Apollo.io** or **ZoomInfo** — email enrichment by domain
7. **Hunter.io** — email pattern discovery by domain
