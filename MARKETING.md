# PracticeAI — Go-To-Market Strategy

## Positioning Statement
PracticeAI is the AI administrative layer for independent medical and dental practices that are losing money to no-shows, claim denials, and patient attrition — and don't have the budget to hire more staff.

**Wedge:** No-show elimination (clearest ROI, easiest sale)
**Expand to:** Insurance verification → Recall automation → Full platform

---

## Target Market (Sacramento / Placer County)

### Primary ICP
- **Who:** Owner-operators of independent medical/dental practices, 1–8 providers
- **Revenue:** $500K–$5M/year
- **Pain intensity:** 15%+ no-show rate OR active billing headaches OR poor recall rates
- **Tech stack:** Already using EHR (Athenahealth, Dentrix, eClinicalWorks, Kareo, or similar)
- **Attitude:** Open to technology, not looking for another app — wants done-for-you
- **Geography:** Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Elk Grove, Rancho Cordova, Rocklin, Lincoln, Auburn

### Secondary ICP
- Practice administrators/office managers at multi-provider practices
- Healthcare groups with 3+ locations looking to standardize operations

### Verticals (Priority Order)
1. Family Medicine / General Practice (highest volume)
2. Dental (high no-show sensitivity, strong recall economics)
3. Chiropractic (high appointment volume, price-sensitive)
4. Pediatrics (parent-scheduling complexity)
5. Dermatology (recall revenue high, discretionary)
6. Mental Health / Therapy (no-shows very costly)

## Pricing Strategy

### Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Solutionreach | $350–550/mo | Reminders only, no verification |
| NexHealth | $500–1,200/mo | Booking-focused, no recall depth |
| Luma Health | $800–1,500/mo | Enterprise-oriented, slow to implement |
| Klara | $250–600/mo | Communication only |
| Manual + staff | $4,000–7,000/mo FTE equiv | Error-prone, no 24/7 coverage |

**PracticeAI position:** Full-stack at $1,999/mo vs. point solutions requiring 3–4 tools at similar combined cost. ROI-guaranteed.

### Pricing Psychology
- Lead with ROI, not features: "Recover $150K+/year for $24K/year"
- Setup fee ($3,000) demonstrates seriousness and filters tire-kickers
- 90-day guarantee removes purchase risk entirely
- Annual pre-pay option: 2 months free (effective ~$1,665/mo)

## Launch Sequence (Days 1–90)

**Days 1–14: Proof of Concept**
- Sign 2–3 beta clients at 50% discount ($999/mo) for 90 days
- Build case studies with permission (specific numbers)
- Get testimonials and before/after data

**Days 15–45: Content Foundation**
- Launch website with ROI calculator
- Publish Month 1 content calendar
- Begin Google Ads (no-show campaign)
- Start LinkedIn outreach to 50 practice owners/week

**Days 46–90: Paid Acquisition**
- Full Google Ads suite running
- Meta campaigns launching
- First partner agreements signed (2–3 billing companies)
- Target: 8–10 paying clients by Day 90

### 90-Day Revenue Target
- 10 clients × $1,999/mo = **$19,990 MRR**
- 10 setup fees × $3,000 = **$30,000 one-time**
- Total 90-day revenue: **~$60,000**
