# PracticeAI — Brand Identity

## Name & Domain
**Brand Name:** PracticeAI
**Domain:** practiceai.com
**Slug:** practice-ai

## Tagline Options
- Primary: **"Run your practice. Let AI handle the rest."**
- Alt 1: "The operating system for modern medical and dental practices."
- Alt 2: "Stop losing money to no-shows, paperwork, and phone tag."

## Brand Promise
PracticeAI eliminates the administrative burden that keeps healthcare providers from doing actual healthcare. No-shows vanish. Insurance verification happens before the patient walks in. Intake is digital. Follow-up is automatic. The front desk finally has capacity to breathe.

## Logo Concept
A stylized "P" formed from a pulse/heartbeat line that transforms into a checkmark at its peak — signaling clinical precision and task completion. Clean, modern, medical-grade. Works in monochrome and in accent colors. The icon functions independently from the wordmark.

## Typography
- **Display/Headlines:** Fraunces (serif with optical sizes — sophisticated, authoritative, unexpected for SaaS)
- **Body/UI:** DM Sans (clean, modern, clinical without feeling cold)
- **Data/Metrics:** DM Mono (precision in numbers)

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Clinical confidence. Like a specialist who knows exactly what they're doing — no fluff, no filler, just the answer.
- **Not:** Corporate healthcare marketing speak ("empowering wellness journeys"). Not startup hype ("disrupt the patient experience").
- **Is:** Direct, credentialed, solutions-first. Speaks to owners, not patients.
- **Key phrases:** "Before the patient walks in," "Recovered in 30 days," "Your staff focuses on care — AI handles the rest."

## Brand Personality
- Precise (not cold)
- Authoritative (not arrogant)
- Efficient (not robotic)
- Caring by proxy (frees providers to actually care)

## Positioning
PracticeAI is the AI practice management layer for medical and dental practices that are drowning in admin. It's not an EMR. It's not a billing company. It's the AI that runs everything between the clinical work — so the clinical team can focus on patients, not paperwork.

**Target:** Private practice owners, office managers, practice administrators in Sacramento/Placer County

**Price:** $1,999/mo | Setup: $3,000

## Key Claims (research-backed)
- 78% of no-shows cite poor reminder systems as contributing factor
- Average medical practice loses $150,000/year to no-shows alone
- Insurance verification errors cause 90% of claim denials
- 4.6 hours/day: average time front desk spends on phone (before AI)
- 2.1 hours saved per day per staff member with AI intake and scheduling
- 340% ROI average in first year
