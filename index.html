Does PracticeAI integrate with my EHR/PMS? +
Yes. We have pre-built integrations with the major systems — Athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Kareo, Office Ally, and 40+ others. For less common systems, we use a secure API bridge that doesn't require any changes to your existing setup. Implementation takes 3–5 business days.
Is this HIPAA compliant? +
Yes, fully. PracticeAI operates as a HIPAA Business Associate. We sign a BAA with every practice. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Patient data is never used to train AI models. We undergo annual third-party security audits. Full documentation available upon request.
How long does setup take? +
Most practices are fully operational within 7–10 business days. Week 1 covers EHR integration, workflow configuration, and staff training (2 hours, done remotely). Week 2 is go-live with a dedicated implementation manager watching your first week of results. We don't consider it "done" until you've seen your first no-show rate report.
What if my patients don't respond to texts? +
We use a multi-channel cascade: SMS first (highest response), then email, then voice call. If a patient doesn't respond to SMS, we escalate automatically. Our system also tracks preference per patient — if someone consistently responds to calls but ignores texts, we learn that. Response rate averages 91% across all channels within 24 hours of appointment day.
